SmartGirlz in ICT. We are a consortium made up of the University of Dar as Salaam ICT Incubator(UDICTI), Youth for Children(Y4C) Innovation Hub, Tanzania with the main aims of supporting and empowering girls and young women in building capacity on ICT.
We aim to bridge the gap between ICT and girls by teaching them the Human Centered Design(HCD) which will help them solve the problems in their society.
That every girl will have access to the digital economy through education, training and technology. ‚ÄúWe believe every girl and young woman has incredible potential and infinite value, regardless of her background, appearance, beliefs and ability. And also bring about problem solvers and critical thinkers in the society.
